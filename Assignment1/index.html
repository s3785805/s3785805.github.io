Project Ideas

Overview

Uber fuel:

Uber fuel: Uber fuel would by just another sub category or extension of the uber family of a transport company using the employees property instead of having to maintain and manage a fleet of their own. Uber fuel in much like the Uber eats in which you take advantage of ubers extensive network of drivers to deliver to you, not food this time but, Petrol. With the advent of the age of automation why should you have to spend your precious time, going out of your way, filling your car up when you could have the petrol brought straight to your door to fill up whenever you need and without the hassle.

Motivation

With Uber’s extraordinary rise in popularity over the past decade it’s amazing that they haven’t ventured further out into other avenues of profit. With over 2 million active drivers worldwide a fleet of that size can accomplish far more than just ride sharing and food delivery. Uber’s main competition due to its extreme size is people driving themselves around. With over 16 million cars in Australia alone that is a large area of the market there just waiting to be serviced. This new plan would now give Uber a revenue stream from both the car-less and the car owners of the world.

Description

Uber fuel would be another app to compliment the Uber and Uber eats apps but instead you would be calling upon the endless fleets of Uber drivers to supply you with a certain amount of petrol for your car. You would firstly have a map of the surrounding area showing all the petrol stations nearby with the prices of the specific fuel types. You could then either choose a specific station for your uber driver to pick up the petrol from due to lower prices or other factors or just send out a request for petrol from any local station. A request would be sent out to any Uber Fuel drivers in the vicinity and they would then travel to the petrol station and purchase and retrieve the fuel, there would have to be a system setup in which petrol stations opt in and are fitted with specific equipment to take in the orders over the app and handle the transaction through that and then give out a certain serial number or QR code to the driver who would then upon arrival at the petrol station show show that to the attendant and be able to receive that fuel that has been paid for. Another factor is that in order to carry the amount of fuel that a person may require for their car the Uber fuel driver may have to get a storage tank installed in their car, most likely the boot, so that they can deliver the fuel from the station to the home of the customer. Once the transaction has gone through at the petrol station and the order has been acquired and stored in the tank in the Uber drivers car they will then proceed to drive to the home or wherever the customer may be, all the while the customer can track the driver and also have access to his information in case anything goes wrong. Much in the same way that an Uber eats driver doesn’t pick up your food and then drive away with it, an Uber Fuel driver would also not do this as they are following very similar routine and any funny business can easily be picked up by the customer who would be tracking the driver and then use the information they have access to to make sure that that driver feels the consequences of their action and the fuel is delivered. Once the Uber driver reaches the destination with the fuel they will then hook up a nozzle to their cargo tank and transfer the petrol from the cargo tank into the customers car. This system would cut out all the unnecessary hassle that is driving to the petrol station, maybe having to wait in line for a free nozzle, standing there and having to wait while it fills up and possibly having to listen to the ads that play over the speaker on the pump. All of it would be done by someone else for a small fee and free up your time for much more important matters.

Tools and Technologies

For this idea to become a reality it will of course need the main app to be programmed, setup and intertwined with the Uber network to allow for it to call on the pre existing fleet of Uber drivers that already exist. It's also going to require the setup of a sort of secondary app for the petrol stations to use to manage and handle the transaction coming from the Uber fuel app requesting a purchase. Another piece of equipment needed is the external petrol tank to be installed in the cars of willing Uber fuel drivers, instead of having to install it themselves Uber could instead just release an Uber fuel package that when you sign up to be an uber fuel driver they send out and the driver can install it themselves, saving labour costs on Uber’s side.

Skills Required

Of course to complete this we will require same very capable programmers able to take the pre-existing code in the Uber and Uber eats apps and write and manipulate it to suit the new layout and objectives of the new app, with very little knowledge of app development in my mind i imagine that this task would be tricky but for any expert programmer the act of taking the apps that already exist and modifying them to suit the new idea shouldn’t be impossible. Another thing that will be required though will be the manufacture or purchase of the tanks to be installed in the uber cars. They would have to be of a certain design that they can fit into any car with ease and car be uninstalled with ease too. This is a tricky task as the answer will probably be to purchase these tanks on bulk instead of getting them specially made but then finding a tank that fits all cars will be a task in and of itself

Outcome

If this project was to be a success then of course we would see yet another avenue of time wasting in our day to day lives vanish and our precious time would become freer once more, not to mention the additional revenue stream coming from an otherwise untapped market. The original problem of wasting time at petrol stations and the necessity of it would be null as no longer would the average man be forced into it as they now have another option. The impact of this project would be small in and of itself but i feel that it’s not the project that makes the difference but more the idea that people will start to think “what else don’t i really need to do anymore” and with that more and more of life's mundane tasks are outsourced to preserve the time and effort of the individual.
